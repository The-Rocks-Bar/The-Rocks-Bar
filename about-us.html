About us

What is the Rocks Bar?

It’s time to get pumped about what we’re all about!

The Rocks Bar is not just a place to get your favourite drink, it's so much more than that. Think of an electric crowd, rocking music, and fans who love hard rock as much as you!

Together, we can rock out to new artists and amp up our shared passion for hard rock. So, join us, because this isn’t just a place , it’s a community where music lovers unite and unforgettable moments are made!

Our Vision for Hard Rock

In a world where hard rock music is not always accessible, we believe in offering opportunities for local talent and fans to enjoy music without prejudice or barriers. This will lead to a future where both artists and fans can express themselves and connect.
